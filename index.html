Crystal Collector

Rules


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Match"
  2. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score(the value is hidden from sight!) .
  3. Match your total score to the random number to Win!
  4. If your total score goes above the random number you lose!
  5. Each time a new round starts, the Number To Match and the values of each crystal will change.
